About me

Hi..! My name is Subiharan. I design and code beautifully simple things, and I love what I do. I have all the skills to create a new user friendly website or improve existing websites for individuals and organizations. I currently work as a freelancer and I am open for new opportunities.

I like to develop expertise in a number of areas over the course of my life. Fueled by high energy levels and boundless enthusiasm, I’m easily inspired and more then willing to follow my fascinations wherever they take me. I’m passionate, expressive, multi-talented spirit with a natural ability to inspire. I’m never satisfied to just come up with ideas. Instead I have an almost impulsive need to act on them.

I am a person with Mediator personality type INFP-T. I’m able to inspire and i am at my best when I’m sharing my creative expressions with others.

Interested in the entire frontend spectrum and working on ambitious projects with positive people.
